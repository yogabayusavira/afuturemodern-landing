What "people-powered" means

Four commitments that define the model.

Earned, not bought

Access is earned through invitation, application and vetting, or contribution to the cooperative's infrastructure. The whitelist is not for sale.

Admin-authored framing

Members submit objective fields — price, timeline, work samples. The cooperative authors positioning so the work speaks for itself, not the cold-pitch voice.

85% to the people who shipped

One 15% house cut funds shared infrastructure. No agency middleman. No stacked platform tax.

Attribution is permanent

The ledger compounds across years. Contributions do not reset to zero when a contract closes.

Provenance · Discernment · Equity

Core values

Provenance

Resources and acknowledgement flow back to original ideas and the labor that moves culture forward. Attribution is permanent.

Discernment

Future Modern curates contributors, vets incoming RFPs, and assembles cross-pillar teams the way a strong agency partner would.

Equity

Eighty-five percent of contract revenue flows to the people who shipped. $BUILD tokens accrue with contribution. The platform is owned by the people building it.

Truth and inquiry

Where we are unsure, we say so. Where we are certain, we ship.
